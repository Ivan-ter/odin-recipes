Tetrazzini

Description

Turkey Tetrazzini is a quick and easy way to turn leftover turkey into a family favorite.

Ingridients

  • 1 (8 ounce) package egg noodles
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 (6 ounce) can sliced mushrooms
  • 2 cups chopped cooked turkey

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add noodles and cook until al dente, 8 to 10 minutes.
  2. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  3. FMeanwhile, melt butter in a large heavy skillet. Drain mushrooms, add to skillet, and cook, stirring, for 1 minute. Stir in turkey, condensed soup, sour cream, salt, and pepper.
